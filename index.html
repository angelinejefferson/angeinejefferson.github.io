It’s time to celebrate the givers!

Thursday Network is pleased to announce the 5th Annual Spirit of Service awards where young leaders and organizations are recognized for their contributions through volunteerism and philanthropy in the Greater Washington DC region. In carrying on the theme of giving, this event will also serve as fundraiser for the I Empower scholarship which helps high school students pursue a college education.

Who are the honorees?

Thursday Network is pleased to announce the 4th Annual Spirit of Service awards where young leaders and organizations are recognized for their contributions through volunteerism and philanthropy in the Greater Washington DC region. Awards will be given in the following categories:

Maudine R. Cooper Community Excellence Award

To be awarded to an exemplary community and corporate partner of the Greater Washington Urban League and Thursday Network.

DMV Luminary Award

A recognition bestowed upon an inspirational young professional that exemplifies the National Urban League creed of Empowering Communities and Changing Lives.

Thursday Network Legacy Circle

To be awarded to past Thursday Network member that has created a lasting legacy through their work with the National Urban League movement and the greater community at large.

Volunteer of the Year

To be awarded to a current Thursday Network member that has devoted their time and resources to Thursday Network throught the year.

What is Thursday Network?

Founded in 1992, Thursday Network is comprised of over 200 young professionals committed to serving the Washington Metropolitan area through community programs and partnership that address the Empowerment Goals of the National Urban League. The mission of TN is to provide a forum for young adults, ages 21 – 40, to focus their energies on community service, professional development, and political involvement.
